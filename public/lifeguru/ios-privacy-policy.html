📋 2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide and improve our services:

A. Account & Authentication Data

  • Mobile Number: Collected solely for secure authentication via OTP (One-Time Password). We do not collect email addresses or passwords for login.
  • Display Name: A name or nickname you provide for personalization within the App.

B. Transaction Data

  • Purchase History: Records of in-app credit pack purchases, including pack name, credits added, and transaction timestamps, stored securely to maintain your credit balance. We do not store your credit card, bank details, or any financial information; all payments are handled securely by Apple.
  • Credit Balance: Your current credit count is stored to manage your usage of the guidance feature.

C. Spiritual Guidance Queries

  • Questions: When you ask for spiritual guidance, your question is sent to our secure backend engine to generate a response. We do not store or retain your questions or the generated guidance responses on our servers. The guidance is delivered to your device in real time and is not persisted after the session ends.

D. Device & Analytics Data

  • Firebase Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data such as app feature usage, session duration, and user engagement patterns to understand how our App is used and to improve the experience.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: We collect anonymized crash reports including device model, iOS version, and stack traces to identify and fix bugs and improve app stability.

🔧 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To securely authenticate you via mobile OTP verification.
  • To deliver personalized spiritual guidance based on the Bhagavad Gita in your chosen language.
  • To manage your in-app credit balance and process credit pack purchases.
  • To provide rewarded ad experiences that allow you to earn free guidance credits.
  • To monitor and improve app stability through crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics).
  • To analyze anonymous usage patterns and improve the user experience (Firebase Analytics).
  • To serve advertisements through Google AdMob.

📢 4. Advertising

LifeGuru displays advertisements through Google AdMob, including rewarded video ads that allow you to earn free guidance credits. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads.

  • iOS Ad Tracking: We comply with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You will be prompted for consent before any tracking for ad personalization occurs.
  • Non-Personalized Ads: If you opt out of tracking, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized based on your data.
  • Rewarded Ads: You may voluntarily watch rewarded video ads to earn free credits. No additional data is collected during this process beyond standard ad interaction metrics.

🤝 5. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication (OTP), secure database hosting (Cloud Firestore), crash reporting (Crashlytics), and usage analytics (Firebase Analytics).
  • Apple: For processing In-App Purchase payments for credit packs.
  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements within the App, subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Service Provider: Your spiritual questions are sent to our secure backend engine to generate personalized guidance. These queries are processed in real time, are not linked to your personal identity, and are not stored on our servers.

🔒 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using TLS/SSL protocols.
  • Secure cloud infrastructure powered by Google Firebase.
  • Authenticated API calls using Firebase ID tokens.
  • No storage of financial payment information on our servers.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

🗑️ 7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: Your account data (name, phone number) and purchase history are retained only while your account is active.
  • No Guidance Storage: Your spiritual guidance queries and generated responses are not stored on our servers. They exist only during your active session.
  • Deletion: You can request Account Deletion at any time from the App Settings. Upon confirmation, all your personal data — including your profile, purchase history, and credit balance — will be permanently removed from our servers immediately.
  • Irreversible: Once your account is deleted, all associated data is purged and cannot be recovered.
