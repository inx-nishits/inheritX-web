Rise 2 Shine

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Rise 2 Shine, developed by Inheritx Solutions ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our specialized productivity and reward application for parents and children on Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our mobile application (the "App").

2. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K Compliance)

We take children's privacy very seriously.

  • Parental Control: The App is completely managed by a parent or legal guardian ("Verified Parental Consent").
  • No Direct Collection from Children: We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All child profiles (names, nicknames, avatars, tasks) are created, managed, and controlled exclusively by the parent account.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not allow third-party advertising networks to track or collect data from child-directed sections of our App.

3. Collection of Your Information

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services:

A. Account & Authentication Data

  • Mobile Number: Collected solely for secure authentication via OTP (One-Time Password). We do not collect email addresses or passwords for login.
  • Parent Profile: Names or nicknames you provide for your parent profile.
  • Security: We store a secured (hashed) 4-digit passcode for parental access control.

B. Child Profile Data

  • Child Info: Nicknames, visuals (avatars), and age (optional) provided by the parent for personalization.

C. Usage & Financial Data

  • Transaction Data: Secure receipts from the Google Play Store to validate your Subscription status (e.g., "Premium Access"). We do not store your credit card or bank details; all financial transactions are handled securely by Google.
  • Analytics: Anonymous device data (model, Android version) to improve app stability.

4. Use of Your Information

  • To securely authenticate you via mobile OTP.
  • To manage your subscription ("Rise 2 Shine Premium").
  • To sync tasks and rewards securely across your family's devices.
  • To send operational notifications (reminders, updates).

5. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted providers:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, secure database hosting, and cloud functions.
  • Google Play: For processing In-App Purchase and subscription payments.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: Data is retained only while your account is active.
  • Deletion: You can request Account Deletion anytime in the App Settings. All personal data will be permanently removed from our servers immediately upon confirmation.
